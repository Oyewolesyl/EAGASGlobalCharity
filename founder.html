Augusta C. Akparanta-Emenogu Founder & Executive Director
Leadership profile

A life of governance, advocacy and community empowerment.

Augusta C. Akparanta-Emenogu is a governance specialist, development practitioner, media advocate and community empowerment leader with more than two decades of experience working to improve the lives of vulnerable individuals and underserved communities.

She is the Founder and Executive Director of EAGAS Global Charity Foundation (EGCF), a nonprofit organization established in 2003 and dedicated to advancing education, advocacy, women empowerment, psychosocial support, humanitarian assistance and sustainable community development initiatives across Nigeria.

Throughout her professional career, she has worked with respected national and international organizations including the British Council, Plan International, USAID and Pact, contributing to governance strengthening, civic engagement, media advocacy, community mobilization and development programming.

Her expertise spans governance and accountability, public finance monitoring, advocacy campaigns, capacity building, women empowerment, participatory learning, civic engagement and social development initiatives that strengthen communities and improve opportunities for vulnerable populations.

Augusta holds a Master's Degree in Political Science and Public Administration from the University of Benin, a Postgraduate Certificate in Public Relations from the Nigerian Institute of Journalism and a Certificate in Women and Information from the University of Hamburg, Germany.

Driven by a passion for social impact and community transformation, she has dedicated her career to empowering women, girls, widows, orphans and vulnerable groups through programs that promote dignity, inclusion, leadership development and sustainable livelihoods.

Under her leadership, EAGAS Global Charity Foundation continues to create pathways to opportunity through advocacy, education, humanitarian support and community-based development initiatives that make lasting differences in people's lives.
