Programs

Integrated initiatives for protection, empowerment and participation.

Our programs combine humanitarian support with training, advocacy, mentorship and community-led development.

Women and Girls Empowerment

Mentorship, skills support, education advocacy and confidence building for women and girls.

  • Objectives: increase safety, confidence, learning and economic participation.
  • Expected outcomes: stronger support networks, improved self-advocacy and practical life skills.
  • Impact examples: mentorship circles, community talks and education-focused outreach.

Widows Support Initiative

Targeted care for widows through relief, psychosocial support, referrals and livelihood pathways.

  • Objectives: reduce isolation and improve household stability.
  • Expected outcomes: stronger access to support, dignity and income opportunities.
  • Gallery area: field photos and beneficiary stories can be added as programs expand.

Orphans Development Program

Support for vulnerable children through education encouragement, mentoring and protection awareness.

  • Objectives: improve belonging, learning continuity and access to care.
  • Expected outcomes: children feel seen, protected and encouraged.
  • Impact examples: outreach visits, school-readiness conversations and child-centered support.

Internally Displaced Persons Support

Dignity-centered support for IDPs and vulnerable families facing displacement, instability or emergency need.

  • Objectives: provide relief, referrals and community reintegration support.
  • Expected outcomes: reduced vulnerability and improved access to essential support.
  • Impact examples: relief outreach, listening sessions and partner referrals.

Micro-Credit and Economic Empowerment

Enterprise readiness, micro-credit education and practical financial guidance for local entrepreneurs.

  • Objectives: improve income resilience and responsible financial participation.
  • Expected outcomes: more beneficiaries able to plan, save, trade and repay responsibly.
  • Impact examples: small business readiness sessions and accountability groups.

Community Development

Participatory projects that respond to local priorities and strengthen community structures.

  • Objectives: support collective problem-solving and inclusive local action.
  • Expected outcomes: more informed communities and stronger development participation.
  • Impact examples: community outreach, mobilization and local consultations.

Media Advocacy and Civic Engagement

Awareness programs covering public finance, budget tracking education, gender advocacy and civic participation.

  • Objectives: help communities understand rights, budgets and accountability channels.
  • Expected outcomes: stronger voices in governance and local decision-making.
  • Impact examples: advocacy campaigns, public finance awareness and budget literacy sessions.

Capacity Building and Participatory Learning

Training for beneficiaries, volunteers and community actors on leadership, service delivery and psychosocial support.

  • Objectives: strengthen skills, confidence and service readiness.
  • Expected outcomes: better equipped community volunteers and program participants.
  • Related areas: fundraising initiatives, psychosocial support and gender advocacy.
