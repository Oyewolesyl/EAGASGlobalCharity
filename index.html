Who we are

A foundation built around dignity, opportunity and practical support.

EAGAS Global Charity Foundation works with vulnerable communities to identify urgent needs, mobilize support and build programs that restore confidence, skills and hope. Our work spans mentorship, advocacy, economic empowerment, civic education and humanitarian relief.
